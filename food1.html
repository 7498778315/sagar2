Bastian at the Top whisks guests away to a rustic cave in arid desert lands

The newly opened restaurant on the 48th floor of Kohinoor Square presents a feast for both stomach and eyes

Bastian At The Top marks a significant culinary milestone as it graces the 48th floor of Mumbai's tallest commercial building.

The restaurant, owned by the esteemed actress Shilpa Shetty Kundra, promises an elevated dining experience that transcends the ordinary. From the breathtaking view of the city's skyline to the opulent interiors, every detail has been meticulously designed to create an unforgettable ambiance.
